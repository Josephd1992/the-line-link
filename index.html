How The Link Works

1. Recording

  • Tap Record a Message and select your agency or "Community Member."
  • Specific prompts will appear. You can record a voice clip OR type a message for one, two, or all three cards.
  • Hit Submit at the bottom when you are finished.
  • Your message is sent to a private queue for supervisor review.

2. The Gallery

  • Tap Listen to the Link to see the shared messages of appreciation.
  • Tap any card in the gallery to expand it and play the audio or read the note.
  • Use the Filters or Random button to find specific messages.

3. Privacy & Safety

  • Messages are reviewed by PVRCC Supervisors before going live to ensure a positive environment.
  • Security: Please do not include active case numbers or sensitive HIPAA data.
